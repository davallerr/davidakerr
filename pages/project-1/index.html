pyramid

Student project merging the visual languages of modern Swiss Style and Ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs. Finding a common ground in the grid, I conceptualized a brand called pyramid, presented as a series of hookah accessory packaging designs.

Shisha & Coals
A bag of shisha fits into the pyramid box, and the obelisk parts at the base to hold a stack of coals.

Ancient Egyptian & Modern Swiss
Leaning on the common ground of a foundational emphasis on organization, I developed a brand that presents Egyptian hieroglyphs in colors and contrast inspired by the Swiss modernist movement.
