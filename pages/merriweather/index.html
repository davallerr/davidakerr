Merriweather

Design of a WordPress theme developed in-house for building and maintaining audiology practice websites. Intended to balance customizability and manageability, the design is modular, color is based on 4 swatch variables, and variation in staff size and number of locations are easily accommodated.

Focused & Adaptable
Using collective expertise in the hearing care industry, Merriweather’s templates and their customization options were designed and built to make creating an effective and personalized website for an audiology practice a simple and efficient process.

User-Minded Information & Interaction
Website content and user interface was designed to encourage exploration and makes informing the user on hearing loss and its treatment a priority.
