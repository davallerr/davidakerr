8-Bit Energy

Student project conceptualizing a brand of energy drink that takes inspiration from video games to promote products that boost different “stats.”

Drink a Potion

Dark & Light
Packaging was created for a sugar-free “light side” version of each flavor, which flips the color palette from the black background of the standard version.
