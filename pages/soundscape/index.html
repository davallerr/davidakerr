Soundscape

Business concept and identity for a youth community place that is centered around music. Products created include stickers, business collateral, identity booklet, and mockups for bus stop posters and pickguards.

Clean Chaos
Soundscape’s brand presents it as an open place for musical expression and exploration. I started with developing a freeform pattern, building off of itself and connecting back inward in different ways.

Sounds & Symbols
Concepts for Soundscape’s interior space featured icons and silhouettes that play with the Soundscape pattern, which also becomes the basis for much of Soundscape’s signage, collateral, and general branding.
