Audiology Profiles

A tool for talking about and building brand style guides for members of a nationwide business management and consulting group for independent audiologists. Several audiologist-specific archetypal profiles were identified and developed to serve as starting points and references to create or strengthen member branding and style guides.

Thorough & Accessible
Helping bridge the gap between the practice owner and their unique brand, the profiles help marketing experts guide the conversation, and help members speak to the character of their practice.

Standard Tools, Unique Brands
Used as a springboard and cross-referenced throughout branding conversations, the brand profiles lead to the member’s singular brand identity, culminating in a style guide for internal and external reference.

Practical Options
To make brand consistency even easier, a single page style sheet template was developed around the same profiles to cover the basics.
