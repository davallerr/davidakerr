Audiology Profiles

A tool for talking about and building brand style guides for members of a nationwide business management and consulting group for independent audiologists. Several audiologist-specific archetypal profiles were identified and developed to serve as starting points and references to create or strengthen member branding and style guides.

Thorough & Accessible
Helping bridge the gap between the practice owner and their unique brand, the profiles help marketing experts guide the conversation, and help members speak to the character or their practice.

Unique & Unified
By running different characters over consecutive months, these news inserts create a unified campaign that develops over the quarter and builds recognition without repetition.

Messaging Across Media
Tools for creating various print and digital pieces cover newspaper ads and inserts, flyers, web ads, social media content, and website banners.

Modular Toolbox
A toolbox outlining different aspects of the campaign makes it simple to create customized Choose Hearing creative of any kind. Messaging of different lengths and audience are easily accessed, as well as every creative asset.
